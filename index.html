Gameplay Overview

A short arc of drafting, events, and encounters that crescendos into the Crypt.

  • Deck start: 22 drafted cards, 16 lands, 1 Scryfall spawn.
  • Loop: event card, encounter, then a Town stop to tune the deck.
  • Goal: clear about four encounters, then face one of 12 Crypt bosses.
