Scoring Formula — How Rank Is Calculated

Leaderboard score combines high-impact unlock counts with current Essence, so long-term progression and active play both matter.

Score = (Achievements × 10,000) + (Buffs × 7,500) + (Tickets × 1,000) + (Brand Ranks × 500) + Essence

Reading The Table — What To Track
  • Score shows overall progression power.
  • Unlocks shows combined achievement, buff, and ticket depth.
  • Brand Ranks is the sum of all Brand ranks owned — each rank is worth 500 pts.
  • Essence is your flexible spend for future upgrades.
