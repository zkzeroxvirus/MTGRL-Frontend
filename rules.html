Core Guide

MTG Roguelike Rules

A custom game mode for Magic: The Gathering. Use the tabs below to navigate: Quick Play covers everything to get a session running, Town details each building and traveler, Achievements lists every unlock and its requirement, and Shop covers all Essence spending options.

Everything you need to get a full run started. Work through each section in order: understand the core rules, build your deck, then jump into the gameplay loop.

📜 Global Rules

These rules apply at all times, in every encounter and phase of the game.

  • Players save their own data. Cheating is not tolerated.
  • Scryfall commands follow the standard Scryfall syntax.
  • Phase+ activates with 3+ players (host draws an extra card and plays an extra land each turn).
  • Gleemax awards 10,000 Essence immediately.
  • Verified hosts may not alter Essence costs, but can add homebrew rules.

📦 Resources

Two currencies power the game. Essence carries over between sessions; XP is earned and spent within a single run.

✨ Essence

Essence is the long-term resource. It persists between sessions and powers pre-game and post-game actions. Essence is intentionally rare and shared across verified hosts.

🔶 XP

XP is your in-run currency. It is used for upgrades, choices, and rewards during the current session only. Unspent XP is lost on death.

📋 Player Limits

Hard limits on what you can have equipped or active at any one time. Keep these in mind when building and upgrading your deck.

Required

1 Commander, 39+ Library.

Maximums

1 Partner, 1 Background, 1 Conspiracy, 1 Vanguard, 1 Emblem, 1 Trinket, 4 Buffs, 1 Ticket.

🎴 Deckbuilding

Build your deck from scratch at the start of each run using random rolls and Scryfall-generated cards. You start with 22 nonland cards, 16 free lands, and one free Scryfall card of your choice.

  1. Roll 5 legal commanders, choose 1.
  2. Generate 22 nonland cards in your color identity. You may keep any, reroll the rest twice, then keep the final set.
  3. Add 16 total lands. Two colors add 2 duals, three colors add 4 duals, four colors add 6 duals, five colors add 8 duals. Fill the rest with basics.
  4. Add one free-choice card (not Sol Ring, not banned, no Game Changers).
  5. Confirm pre-game actions, buffs, tickets, and bonuses before starting.

🎲 Gameplay Loop

Each round follows a fixed sequence. On round 1 only, players choose between a Trinket and an Event. Every round after that is always an Event. Win encounters to earn XP and push toward the Crypt.

Step 1 — Event or Trinket (Round 1 Only)

On the very first round, players vote for an Event or a Trinket. All subsequent rounds are always Events. Resolve, then continue.

Step 2 — Encounter Draw

Host pulls a random encounter. Teams roll d20 to pick turn order.

Death

Lose half XP (rounded down to nearest 5). If all die, return to Town.

Victory

XP equals host starting HP plus bonuses. Choose to Stay Out or Go to Town.

🏘️ Town

Heal to full, gain a Traveler, and perform Town Actions.

🏕️ Staying Out

Keep current HP and gain a permanent increase to Town Actions.

📘 Turn Structure (Party vs Host)

The party acts as a single player against the host. Coordination during the party turn is key to survival.

🛡️ Party Turn

All players act as one. One untap, upkeep, draw, main phase, combat, main phase 2, and end step. Coordinate sequencing.

👹 Host Turn

Host plays normally. Host chooses attacks. Adjacent players may block for the attacked player.

Extra turns belong only to the player who cast the card. In Crypt, the host always starts first.

Phase+: When there are 3 or more players, the host draws an extra card and plays an extra land each turn.

🔥 Host Scaling & Affixes

Hosts grow stronger after every victory. Each affix adds a new ability and increases the XP reward for the party.

Each host victory grants +10 max HP and 1 affix. Duplicate affixes reroll.

🟦 Tier 1 Affixes (+5 XP reward)
  • +1/+1 Counters, Demonstrate, First Strike, Flying, Menace, Prismatic
  • Rebound, Vigilance
🟧 Tier 2 Affixes (+10 XP reward)
  • -1/-1 Counters, Aether Anomaly, Commanding Presence
  • Deathtouch, Haste, Hero Power, Pack Back Up, Setup Battle, Trample
🟥 Tier 3 Affixes (+15 XP reward)
  • Emblem Battle, Double Strike, Hexproof, Indestructible
  • Infect, Training Grounds, Lifelink, Vanguard Battlements

🕱️ Progression to the Crypt

Win enough encounters to unlock the Crypt — the final boss of a run. Each victory also earns loot for your party.

Encounters reward XP equal to host starting HP plus affixes. After each win, the host draws Cash Outs equal to the number of players. Players pick one reward. The defeated deck adds loot cards equal to the number of players.

🧿 Trinkets

Trinkets are persistent items that provide ongoing benefits throughout the run. On round 1 only, players may vote to take a Trinket instead of an Event. Trinkets can be party-wide or personal.

Trinket List
  • Aegis of Verix — Commander blocks can conjure a 4/4 Dragon token once per fight.
  • Aspect of Malfegor — Sacrifice a non-token permanent once per encounter for a scaled reward.
  • Bag of Grain — Up to 4 servings, each grants +3 max HP for the rest of the run.
  • Blade of the Archmagus — Non-creature spells targeting opponents deal 2 damage to creature or host.
  • Campfire — End step: one player gains 2 HP and may discard to draw.
  • Cannon — Sacrifice a non-token artifact once per party turn to deal damage.
  • Celestine, Hama's Light — Commander gains lifelink and cost reduction on friendly targeting spells.
  • Dragovokia — Once per town, roll d6; town actions cost that much less (min 1).
  • Garruk's Clipper — Upkeep: target creature gains +2/+0 and deathtouch.
  • Gideon's Training Saber — Commander gains +1/+0 and first strike.
  • Hu-Blow, the Good — Once per turn, a party member may cast a creature as though it had flash.
  • Loch Shield — At fight start, target commander gains 2 shield counters.
  • Ludwig's Holy Blade / Sal Bidard — Choose one at fight start; grant hexproof or indestructible.
  • Marbles — Instant or sorcery casts create a random 1/1 token.
  • Mitsy, Abandoned — Fight start: target player gains a Marauder's Axe.
  • Mirror Shield — Commander gains +0/+5 and reflects combat damage to an opponent.
  • Oppressive Skeleton / Aggressive Skeleton — Choose a 1/1 regenerate or 4/1 menace skeleton.
  • Shovel Pile — Once per town roll d20 for XP or a random trinket.
  • Sunforged Slicer — Combat damage lets you discard up to 2 and draw that many.
  • Thirsting Axe — Combat: deal 1 damage to party creatures; gains counters for each death.
  • Tonitrus, Skullduster — Upkeep: power 4+ creature gains +3/+0 and trample.

  • Vogar, Bound for Death — Sacrifice creatures to create 6/5 zombies.
  • Walking Corpse — Fight start: target player gains a 1/2 Zombie with persist.
  • Watcher's X Eye — X spells cost 1 less for the party.

⚔️ The Crypt

The final challenge of a run. The Crypt boss is a high-power deck that always takes the first turn. Victory earns major Essence rewards.

Entering

Triggered at 50 HP or by choice. Host pulls a random boss.

Rules

Crypt starts at 50 HP and always takes the first turn.

Crypt bosses are brutal, high-power decks with extra boosts. Expect a challenge.

❌ Losing

Gain Essence equal to total mana value of deck + sideboard.

🏆 Winning

Gain 500 Essence, a Crypt Buff, and Essence from deck value.

After each encounter you can return to Town to heal, shop, and trade. You start with 2 Town Actions per visit; Staying Out adds +2 each time. Every action has an XP cost that must be fully paid. Travelers and Cash Outs are free and do not use a Town Action.

🏘️ Town Locations

Each location offers a unique service. Visit as many as your Town Actions allow.

Town Locations
  • ⚒️ Blacksmith — Upgrade (25 XP) or Augment (50 XP)
  • 🎲 Casino — 10 XP + a card to roll d20
  • 🏰 Castle — 10 XP to roll for commanders
  • ⛪ Cathedral — describe a card; add to deck or destroy for Essence
  • 🏦 Bank — move up to 6 cards between deck and sideboard
  • 🛒 Merchant — buy packs (Mystery, ID, Pro, Set, Mythic, OTAG)
  • 🔮 Mystic — destroy a card for a Pro Pack equal to its mana value
  • 🌀 Portal — buy Temporal token copies (up to 15 XP)
  • 🍻 Tavern — swap cards and lands
  • 📦 Trading Post — trade twice per town

🌟 Travelers

A random Traveler arrives in Town after each encounter. Interacting with them is free and does not cost a Town Action.

Traveler Options

  • Cats in a Coat, Centaur Scouter, Centepoid Warrior
  • Dwarven Artificer, Elven Demonologist, Fungal Lich
  • Giff Bandito, Hell's Librarian, Hollyphant
  • Kimi the Cat, Silly the Jester, The Trader
  • Vanguard Mercenaries, Wandering Card Merchant, Zorbo

💎 Cash Outs

Cash Outs are bonus rewards drawn after each encounter victory. They are claimed in Town and are free — they do not cost a Town Action.

Available Rewards
  • Alpha Pack, Artifact Packs, Artifact Pro Packs
  • Bonus Bank, Cathedral, Mystic, Tavern, Trading Post
  • Enchantment Packs, ID Packs, Extra Town Action
  • Free Augment, Free Casino, Free Castle, Free Portal
  • Free Upgrade, Mono Packs, Mono Pro Packs
  • Mystery Pack, Mythic Pack, Planeswalker Packs
  • Utility Land Pack

Earn Achievements by completing special challenges during your runs. Each Achievement unlocks a permanent bonus that carries into future sessions. Color Combo Tickets require beating the Crypt with a specific strategy; Land and Misc Achievements have their own unique conditions.

✨ Achievements & Buffs

Unlock permanent bonuses by meeting each achievement's requirement. The bonus is active from your very next run once earned.

Color Combination Tickets
  • Azorius — Law of Efficiency — W: gain 1 life when casting on another player's turn. U: draw when you counter a spell or ability.
  • Boros — Charge of Conviction — W: untap a creature when you attack. R: solo attacker gains +2/+0.
  • Dimir — Whisper Network — U: untap a nonland when casting on opponents' turns. B: exile a card when you target a permanent you do not control.
  • Golgari — Cycle of Rot — B: -1/-1 counter on a creature when your permanent dies. G: create a Food token when a creature dies.
  • Gruul — Primal Fury — R: give haste when a creature becomes modified. G: give trample when a creature attacks.
  • Izzet — Experimental Sparks — U: scry 1 then draw 1 on instant cast. R: deal 1 damage on sorcery cast.
  • Orzhov — Tithe and Toil — W: populate on token entry. B: create a 1/1 Zombie when a creature you control dies.
  • Rakdos — Showstopper's Encore — B: draw 1 and lose 1 life when a creature dies. R: create a Treasure on combat damage to an opponent.
  • Selesnya — Harmony's Bloom — W: +1/+1 counter when you gain life. G: gain 1 life when you cast a creature.
  • Simic — Adaptive Pattern — U: +1/+1 counter when you draw your second card. G: draw when a +1/+1 counter is placed.
Land Achievements
  • Nature's Blessing — Replace duals with Triomes. Requirement: beat a Crypt with 50+ lands.
  • Dawn of Crabs — Start with a Plains. Requirement: beat a Crypt with a Crab commander.
  • Fish's Pond — Start with an Island. Requirement: beat a Crypt with a Fish commander.
  • Scorpion's Nest — Start with a Swamp. Requirement: beat a Crypt with a Scorpion commander.
  • Raccoon's Rage — Start with a Mountain. Requirement: beat a Crypt with a Raccoon commander.
  • Horse's Gallop — Start with a Forest. Requirement: beat a Crypt with a Horse commander.
  • Construct's Salvation — Start with a Wastes. Requirement: beat a Crypt with a Construct commander.
  • Changeling's LandForm — Start with a random basic land. Requirement: beat a Crypt with a Changeling commander.
Miscellaneous Achievements
  • Booster Tutor — Add Booster Tutor at deckbuilding. Requirement: reach 250 XP in a session.
  • Compelling Madness — Once per encounter, gain 5 life at instant speed. Requirement: indirectly kill a non-host player.
  • Dog's Best Friend — Start with a random Companion. Requirement: beat a Crypt with a Companion.
  • Gambler's Never Quit — Coin flip for first town action cost. Requirement: win six coin flips in a row.
  • Happy Fun Land — Start with 5 Attractions. Requirement: beat a Crypt with Attractions.
  • One With Death — Gain a second free card; one may be a Game Changer. Requirement: beat a Crypt on turn one.
  • Stick It To Me — Start with 5 Sticker Sheets. Requirement: beat a Crypt with Stickers.
  • Victory Lap — Buff maximum increases to 7. Requirement: beat 3 Crypt bosses in one session.

Spend Essence to improve your starting position before a run or to unlock permanent upgrades after one. Pre-game options apply to the current run only; post-run options persist into future sessions.

🎴 Pre-Game Purchases

Spend Essence before deckbuilding begins to customize your starting conditions for this run.

  • 🎲 0 Essence — Commander Gamble (mulligan commander, next Castle is free)
  • 👪 35 Essence — Partner/Background option (random from 5)
  • 🌍 35 Essence — Land Replacement (swap a basic for a dual or Triome)
  • 🔁 50 Essence — Commander Mulligan (new 5 commander options)
  • 🔄 50 Essence — Card Mulligans (2 extra non-commander mulligans)
  • 🎭 75 Essence — Identity Trickster (add/remove a commander color)
  • 🌌 150 Essence — Leyline Addition (does not count toward starting 39)

  • 2000 Essence — Color Combo Ticket
  • 2500 Essence — Trinket Ticket
  • 4000 Essence — Conspiracy Ticket
  • 5000 Essence — Vanguard Ticket
