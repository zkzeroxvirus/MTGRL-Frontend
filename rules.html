ğŸ´ Deckbuilding

  1. Roll 5 legal commanders, choose 1.
  2. Generate 22 nonland cards in your color identity. You may keep any, reroll the rest twice, then keep the final set.
  3. Add 16 total lands. Two colors add 2 duals, three colors add 4 duals, four colors add 6 duals, five colors add 8 duals. Fill the rest with basics.
  4. Add one free-choice card (not Sol Ring, not banned, no Game Changers).
  5. Confirm pre-game actions, buffs, tickets, and bonuses before starting.

ğŸ“˜ Turn Structure (Party vs Host)

ğŸ›¡ï¸ Party Turn

All players act as one. One untap, upkeep, draw, main phase, combat, main phase 2, and end step. Coordinate sequencing.

ğŸ‘¹ Host Turn

Host plays normally. Host chooses attacks. Adjacent players may block for the attacked player.

Extra turns belong only to the player who cast the card. In Crypt, the host always starts first.

Entering

Triggered at 50 HP or by choice. Host pulls a random boss.

Crypt starts at 50 HP and always takes the first turn.

Crypt bosses are brutal, high-power decks with extra boosts. Expect a challenge.
